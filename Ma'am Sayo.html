Engr. Maria Rizette H. Sayo serves as a dedicated Full-Time Faculty Instructor at Adamson University, embodying a strong commitment to the field of computer engineering. Engaged in both academic and professional circles, she is an active Member of the Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines. Furthermore, Engr. Sayo is a proud Member of the Leadership in Education Academy and Development Philippines, showcasing her holistic approach to education and professional development. Her educational journey includes pursuing a Master of Engineering with Specialization in Computer Engineering at PLM, complementing her foundational Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering earned from PLM in 2005. Engr. Sayo's dual commitment to education and professional involvement reflects her dedication to advancing the field of computer engineering at Adamson University and within broader professional networks.
