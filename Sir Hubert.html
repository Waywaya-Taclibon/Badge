Eng. Hubert Q. Temprosa, an esteemed Full-Time Faculty and Associate Professor at Adamson University, exemplifies a profound commitment to both academia and professional leadership. Serving as the President of the Adamson University Alumni Association Inc. (AUAAI), he is dedicated to fostering connections among alumni. Eng. Temprosa is a Lifetime Member of the Institute of Computer Engineers of the Philippines and actively contributes to the fields of mechatronics and robotics as a member of the Mechatronics and Robotics Society of the Philippines. His academic journey includes ongoing doctoral studies in Management at Adamson University, complemented by the attainment of Professional Educational Units in 2013. Eng. Temprosa holds a Master of Science in Management Engineering (2003) and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (2000), both from Adamson University. For inquiries or collaboration, he can be reached at hubert.temprosa@adamson.edu.ph. Eng. Temprosa's combination of academic prowess and leadership roles underscores his significant impact on the realms of education and professional associations in the Philippines.
